Behind the clicks

These pictures are inspired by the things we see all the time in our lives; cats, people, sunsets, sunrise, flowers, etc. It captures the simplicity of life and its wonder within of itself, its cruelty and beauty can be captivating to people who stop and admire its beauty.

About the Photographer

Peter Duane is a Creative Computing graduate, growing up in the Philippines, he had always appreciated the small things he sees. As a child with big dreams, its these small events that mattered to him. Such as petting cats, taking a walk, and basking in the sun.

He believes that things should be taken slowly and appreciate the things around us, take a picture or two, to remember that you have lived that moment, not just as a memory, but also a reminder.
